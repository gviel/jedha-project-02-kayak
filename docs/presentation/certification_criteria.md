Les critères d'évaluation de l'examen de la crtification pour le projet Kayak sont les suivants:

The proposed infrastructure for storing, collecting and making available data meets the needs defined in the specifications and is efficient:
  - Simplicity of the proposed infrastructure scheme
  - Storage capacity of the proposed infrastructure scheme (can accommodate Big Data)
  - Optimization of the construction costs of the proposed infrastructure scheme
  - Quality of the data extracted from the web to the Data lake
  - Accessibility of the data available in the Data Warehouse
  - Robustness and efficiency of the ETL process built
